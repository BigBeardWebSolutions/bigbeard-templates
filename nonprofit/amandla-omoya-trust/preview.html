About Us

Who We Are

Our trust was founded to support community development through sustainable initiatives. We channel resources into upliftment programmes, education, and enterprise development to create lasting impact in the communities we serve. As an autonomous body, we are guided by a board of trustees committed to operating transparently, ethically, and effectively.

Read More

Our Focus

Market and Enterprise Development

We support the growth of small businesses, fostering economic activity within our immediate communities.

Youth Education and Life Skills

Our initiatives aim to empower youth to overcome generational poverty. We place special emphasis on peer-driven support and encouraging beneficiaries to reinvest their knowledge and skills back into their communities.

Food Security

We back local nutrition programmes that assist vulnerable groups, including children and the elderly. Our priority lies with initiatives that grow food within the community or source supplies from small local vendors.

Giving to the Community

What We Do

We fund community-led projects by awarding grants to organisations through an open and competitive application process. This ensures that funding is directed towards the most impactful and sustainable initiatives in the region.
